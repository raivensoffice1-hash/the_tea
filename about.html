About CHAI GALI

Brewing Premium Tea Since Day One

Our Story

CHAI GALI was born from a simple passion - the love for authentic, premium tea. Founded in 2020, our tea outlet quickly became a favorite spot for tea enthusiasts and casual drinkers alike.

We believe that every cup of tea tells a story. Our journey started with a vision to bring the finest quality tea leaves, fresh ingredients, and traditional brewing methods to our community. From sourcing the best teas from renowned gardens to perfecting our recipes, every step is taken with care and passion.

Our Mission

To serve the most authentic, freshly brewed premium teas using traditional methods and the finest ingredients. We're committed to creating a warm, welcoming space where people can connect, relax, and enjoy the perfect cup of tea.

Why Choose CHAI GALI?

  • Premium Quality: Hand-picked tea leaves sourced directly from renowned gardens across India
  • Fresh & Hot: Every cup is freshly brewed to order, served hot and ready
  • Traditional Methods: We use time-honored brewing techniques to extract maximum flavor and aroma
  • Affordable Pricing: Premium quality at prices everyone can enjoy
  • Hygiene & Cleanliness: We maintain the highest standards of hygiene in our kitchen
  • Cozy Ambiance: The perfect place for meetings, hangouts, and peaceful moments

Our Values

Quality

We never compromise on quality. Only the finest ingredients make it to our cups.

Trust

We've earned the trust of thousands of customers through consistent quality and service.
